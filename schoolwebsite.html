AI Learning Assistant

Explain the Pythagorean theorem like I'm a 5th grader.

Imagine you're building a fence, and you want to make sure the corners are perfect right angles. The Pythagorean theorem helps with that! It says that in a right triangle (one with a 90° corner), if you make squares on each side, the area of the square on the longest side (hypotenuse) equals the sum of the areas of the other two squares. In math terms: a² + b² = c².
